# Sample documents

Fictional documents for trying the screening flow end to end.

- `sample-pitch-deck.pdf` — a pitch deck for "Reflex AI" containing a traction
  claim ("10,000 active users") that the Trust Score will flag as contradicted
  against the company's public repository signals.
- `sample-resume.pdf` — a founder resume; extraction keeps career signal only
  (the contact details inside are fictional and demonstrate PII exclusion).

Upload both on the Apply page (Founder view), then switch to VC view and open
the founder's memo to see extraction, claim verification, citations, and the
validator in action.
